Weather App

Weather app tracks the user location and shows the weather data all over the world, using openweathermap.org API.
By default it displays the local weather at the geo-point where you are. Also you can use search form to find the current weather at a specific city. The app displays forecast by days/hours and shows last updated time, temperature, brief description, humidity, wind direction, shows an icon based on the weather. Temperature is represented in Celsius and Fahrenheit units.
